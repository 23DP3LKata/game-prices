Game Overview

Cyberpunk 2077 thrusts players into Night City's vertical sprawl, balancing cinematic quests with fluid first-person gunplay and hacking. The Phantom Liberty expansion adds Dogtown's espionage arc, overhauled perks, and vehicle combat to round out V's climb.

Patch 2.0 reworks cyberware limits, crowd AI, and law enforcement, making stealth and high-threat firefights far more dynamic. Cross-progression links PC and console saves, while DLSS 3.5 and FSR 3 keep ray-traced visuals performant on modern hardware.
