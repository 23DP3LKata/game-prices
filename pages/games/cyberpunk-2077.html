About Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 drops players into the bustling sprawl of Night City, balancing branching storylines with fluid first-person combat. Phantom Liberty expands the experience with the Dogtown district, revamped perk trees, and vehicle combat.

Update 2.0 refreshes police behavior, adds cyberware capacity limits, and overhauls enemy AI for more reactive firefights. Cross-progression supports seamless saves across PC and consoles, while NVIDIA DLSS 3.5 keeps ray-traced visuals crisp.
