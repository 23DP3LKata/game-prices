Seasonal Rewards

PUBG: Battlegrounds operates on a free-to-play model with a purchasable Survivor Pass each season. Bundled G-Coin packs frequently go on sale alongside new map drops.

  • Survivor Pass: €9.99 for the base tier, €29.99 for premium bundle.
  • G-Coin packs discount between 10–20% during Steam seasonals.
  • Event passes roll into the in-game store 2 weeks before season end.
