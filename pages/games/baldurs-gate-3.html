About Baldur's Gate 3

Baldur's Gate 3 adapts the Dungeons & Dragons 5e ruleset into a lavish narrative adventure where party composition, dice rolls, and exploration all matter. Origin characters, co-op multiplayer, and near total system reactivity fuel high replay value.

Larian continues to refine performance, add epilogue content, and experiment with mod support via their community workshop. Split-screen co-op recently launched on Xbox Series consoles after optimizations.
