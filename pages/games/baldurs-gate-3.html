Game Overview

Baldur's Gate 3 adapts the Dungeons & Dragons 5e ruleset into a reactive narrative epic. Origin companions, branching romances, and cooperative story beats make each long campaign feel personal, whether solo or in four-player online co-op.

Constant Larian patches add new epilogues, honor mode variants, and performance boosts. Mod support is expanding via the official toolkit, while split-screen now runs across Series X|S after extensive optimization.
