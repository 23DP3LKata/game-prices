Game Overview

Descend into the decaying kingdom of Hallownest, where labyrinthine tunnels, hidden bosses, and haunting characters reward careful exploration. Charms encourage experimentation with nail arts, spellcasting, and mobility builds.

Team Cherry's free content packs—Hidden Dreams, Grimm Troupe, Lifeblood, and Godmaster—add challenge pantheons, new music, and bosses. Community-made mods and randomizers continue to expand the postgame.
