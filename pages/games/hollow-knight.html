About Hollow Knight

Hollow Knight's labyrinthine kingdom of Hallownest offers layered platforming challenges, hidden bosses, and rich environmental storytelling. Charms allow deep build customization, supporting everything from spell-focused runs to nail-only mastery.

The Lifeblood, Hidden Dreams, and Godmaster updates added new bosses, music, and challenge gauntlets for free. Community mod tools extend replay value with custom areas and quality-of-life enhancements.
