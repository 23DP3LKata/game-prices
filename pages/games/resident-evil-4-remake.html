About Resident Evil 4 Remake

The remake of Resident Evil 4 keeps the original's pacing while enhancing mechanics with parries, stealth, and expanded companion AI. The Separate Ways DLC, Mercenaries mode, and weekly challenge rotations provide strong replay value.

Capcom's RE Engine delivers ray-traced lighting on PC and next-gen consoles. Accessibility presets, gyro aiming on PlayStation, and optional aim assist help new players acclimate without diluting the tension.
