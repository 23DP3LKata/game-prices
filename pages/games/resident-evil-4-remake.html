Game Overview

Leon S. Kennedy's mission to rescue the president's daughter leads deep into a haunted corner of rural Spain, where cult rituals, improvised barricades, and relentless villagers demand poise under pressure. Every encounter leans on deliberate combat rhythms, from perfectly timed knife parries to crowd-control decisions that keep ammo and nerves intact.

The remake relies on the RE Engine for dynamic weather, ray-traced lighting, and expressive character performances. Accessibility presets, gyro aiming, and PS VR2 support round out a package that respects the original while reshaping it for modern platforms.
