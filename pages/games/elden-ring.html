About Elden Ring

Elden Ring combines Souls-like combat with a vast, interconnected world filled with dungeons, legacy castles, and hidden storylines. The Shadow of the Erdtree expansion extends the narrative into the Land of Shadow with new bosses and build options.

Summon co-op allies, invade as a red phantom, or experiment with hundreds of weapon skills. Ongoing balance updates from FromSoftware keep PVP metas shifting while preserving the challenge.
