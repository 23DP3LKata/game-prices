Game Overview

Elden Ring blends Soulsborne combat with a sprawling Lands Between to explore at your pace. Legacy dungeons, field bosses, and layered questlines encourage experimentation with hundreds of weapons, Ashes of War, and spirit summons.

The Shadow of the Erdtree expansion opens the Land of Shadow with new legacy dungeons, incantations, and talismans, providing late-game build options. Balance patches continue to refresh PvP metas and co-op strategies.
