About Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 chronicles Arthur Morgan's struggle as the Van der Linde gang faces the end of the outlaw era. The campaign transitions seamlessly into Red Dead Online, where players form posses, run moonshine operations, and take on legendary bounty hunts.

The PC release adds higher fidelity textures, enhanced draw distances, photo mode, and support for ultra-wide monitors. Rockstar's regular events rotate bonuses across roles, keeping the frontier economy active.
