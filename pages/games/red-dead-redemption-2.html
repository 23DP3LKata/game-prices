Game Overview

Red Dead Redemption 2 follows Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang as they race ahead of encroaching modernity. Story missions bleed seamlessly into an open world of dynamic encounters, simulated wildlife, and emergent side stories that react to your honor and choices.

The PC release adds higher draw distances, richer textures, HDR support, and Rockstar Editor tools, while Red Dead Online evolves with new frontier pursuits and rotating weekly events. Seasonal bonuses, treasure maps, and limited-time clothing drops keep the multiplayer frontier fresh.
