Battle Pass & Bundles

Dota 2 itself is free, but seasonal Battle Passes and Compendium bundles often include exclusive cosmetics and levels. Battle Pass pricing usually starts at €9.99 with laddered bundles scaling to level 50 and beyond.

  • Battle Pass discounts appear near the end of major events.
  • Regional bundles can include Arcana sets with dynamic pricing.
  • Track the Steam Points Shop for limited avatars and effects.

Ranked Season Insights

MMR Resets

Valve typically resets calibration at the start of each ranked season. Keep an eye on the calendar to prepare for queue spikes.

Esports Tie-ins

When Major tournaments launch, event-specific treasure chests and supporter bundles hit the store with short-lived discounts.

New Player Mode

Returning players can take advantage of guided matches and free hero tokens. Strategize purchases around the hero rotation for your role.
