Game Overview

ARC Raiders drops squads into a stylized retro-future where humanity scraps for survival against orbital ARC invasions. Grapple hooks, improvised launch pads, and physics-driven weapons let raiders slingshot around Derbia while salvaging gear for the next push.

Seasonal operations, shared social hubs, and a flexible crafting loop keep progression steady. Embark rotates new ARC threat types, limited-time missions, and marathon expeditions so veteran fireteams always have a fresh gauntlet.
