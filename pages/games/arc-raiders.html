About ARC Raiders

ARC Raiders drops squads into a stylized retro-future where humanity is locked in a desperate struggle against mechanized invaders from orbit. The game blends physics-driven combat with vertical traversal, letting raiders slingshot, grapple, and improvise weaponry while rescuing settlers across Derbia.

The live-service structure layers seasonal operations, modular gear crafting, and shared hubs to coordinate community goals. Frequent updates introduce new ARC threat types, limited-time missions, and long-form expeditions geared toward endgame fireteams seeking tougher challenges.
