Game Overview

HELLDIVERS 2 evolves the top-down cult hit into a third-person co-op shooter with friendly fire chaos. Live major orders shift the galactic front hourly, while stratagems let squads call down railcannons, mechs, and supply pods.

Cross-play between PC and PS5 keeps lobbies full. Arrowhead's free content updates add enemy factions, planetary hazards, and battle passes that never expire, ensuring Super Earth has fresh objectives.
