About HELLDIVERS 2

HELLDIVERS 2 expands the top-down original into a fully third-person cooperative shooter with an emphasis on team fire discipline. Planetary campaigns evolve hourly, and community-driven major orders reshape the galaxy each weekend.

Cross-play squads unlock stratagems that summon orbital strikes, deploy mechs, and reinforce fallen troopers. Arrowhead continues to add enemy factions, experimental weaponry, and seasonal battle passes that never expire, keeping super earth's war effort sustainable for newcomers.
