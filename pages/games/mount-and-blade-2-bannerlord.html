About Bannerlord

Bannerlord deepens the Mount & Blade formula with multi-layered siege tactics, diplomacy, and clan management across the continent of Calradia. Players recruit and train companions, broker alliances, and develop towns through layered economic systems.

Seasonal patches add new cultures, modding hooks, console parity updates, and the long-requested battle terrain system. An active mod scene provides overhauls, total conversions, and quality-of-life tweaks that keep sandbox runs fresh.
