Game Overview

Bannerlord evolves the Mount & Blade formula with layered siege systems, large-scale cavalry skirmishes, and kingdom stewardship ripe for emergent storytelling. Recruit companions, assemble war parties, and leverage smithing, trade, and diplomacy to expand your clan.

Frequent patches and a thriving mod ecosystem add balance tweaks, troop trees, and total conversion experiences. Whether you're building a mercenary empire or scripting a roleplay dynasty, Calradia is designed to feel reactive and persistent.
